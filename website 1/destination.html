Welcome to Raigad

Discover the Beauty of Raigad

Raigad Fort, a historic hill fort in Maharashtra, is known for its rich history, stunning views, and impressive architecture. It served as the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The fort is accessible by a ropeway or a climb of approximately 1737 steps.

Highlights

  • Capital of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's kingdom
  • Located at 2,700 ft above sea level in the Sahyadri range
  • Accessible via 1,700 steps or ropeway
  • Major trekking and heritage tourism spot

Welcome to Rajgad

Discover the Beauty of Rajgad

Rajgad Fort served as the first capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Nestled in the Sahyadri mountain range at an elevation of about 4,300 feet, the fort is known for its majestic presence and historical importance.

Highlights

  • Former capital of the Maratha Empire before Raigad
  • Built on a hill 4,300 ft high in the Sahyadris
  • Shivaji Maharaj spent over 25 years here
  • Rich in history, architecture, and Maratha valor
  • Surrounded by lush greenery and mountain views

Welcome to Panhala

Discover the Beauty of Panhala

Panhala Fort, located near Kolhapur in Maharashtra, is one of the largest and most historic forts in the region. Built during the 12th century and later ruled by the Marathas, it played a key role in the life of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The fort is known for its massive fortifications, hidden tunnels, and scenic surroundings. It was the site of the famous escape of Shivaji Maharaj from Siddi Johar's siege, showcasing his strategic brilliance. Today, Panhala is a popular destination for history lovers and offers beautiful views of the Sahyadri ranges.

Highlights

  • Located near Kolhapur in Maharashtra
  • One of the largest forts in the Deccan region
  • Built in the 12th century by the Shilahara dynasty
  • ater an important Maratha stronghold under Shivaji Maharaj
  • Features massive stone walls, bastions, and secret tunnels
